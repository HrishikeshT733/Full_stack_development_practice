Lokmanya Tilak

Early Life Political Career

Early Life

Lokmanya Bal Gangadhar Tilak, born on 23 July 1856 in Ratnagiri, was a prominent freedom fighter and social reformer. He is considered the "Father of the Indian Independence Movement."

Political Career

Tilak was one of the first leaders of the Indian independence movement. He popularized the slogan "Swaraj is my birthright and I shall have it."

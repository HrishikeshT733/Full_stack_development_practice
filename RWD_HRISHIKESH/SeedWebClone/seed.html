NSDC Instruction

Dear Students,

Your courseware is locked by the system. To unlock your courseware, kindly fill NSDC Registration.

  • Candidate Registration Process
  • Step 1: The candidate will have to visit the Skill India Digital from their browser (https://www.skillindiadigital.gov.in/home).
  • Step 2: Then the user needs to click on the Register button in the top right corner.
  • Step 3: The user needs to click on the Learner option.
  • Step 4: In the next screen, the user needs to enter the 10-digit Mobile Number from which they want to register on Skill India.
